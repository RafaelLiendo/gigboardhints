GIGBOARDHINTS

The Headrush Gigboard is a great pedalboard with a lot of great amplifier, cabinet, microphone and effects models. But we have all at some point wondered what were the real pieces of gear that inspired the ones we have inside the Gigboard.

This website is intended as a little guide to the gear and presets we have available inside the Gigboard, and it is a compilation of information found all over the internet.
